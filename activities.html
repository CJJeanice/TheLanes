Activities at The Lanes

Unit Bowl

All active duty units are eligible for one hour of free bowling at the bowling center. Advance registration is required. If you would like to book your unit for an hour of bowling please call the center. The unit can extend the hours for extra payment of $10 per hour per lane if they would like to stay for more than one hour of free bowling, no charges on the shoes.

Specto

Specto uses a lidar sensor that reads up to 6 lanes. It gathers about 120 readings per lane per shot. It then uses this information to calculate about 40 different readings. In our center we have 2 of this spactacular device to assure all our customers can be there best during competition.

Torch

The Kegel Torch is a simple light targeting tool that be installed in bowling centers to help bowlers with accuracy and alignment. We offer this technology with to our customers whenever they would like to improve targeting and accuracy when it comes to playing different patterns.

Their background ranges from military personel, civilian/government contractors, and some are even PBA bowlers willing to help children improve the game of bowling.

Leagues

A bowling League is a group event where several teams bowl against each other over the course of a season. Most bowling leagues consist of teams of 3-5 players that meet up once a week or once every other week, usually at the same day and time. Leagues can be set up as male-only, female-only, or mixed.
