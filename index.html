Enjoy Bowling with Family and Friends!

The Lanes at Fort Meade offer special well-designed amenities such as a pro shop, meeting room, and game room creating an inviting atmosphere for the customers. The facility offers 36 lanes for customers to enjoy alongside our other amenities. We offer different types of deals and Packages under the Party Packages and A La Carte on our page that every bowler would love:

  • We are one of few bowling alleys nearby that offers Specto bowling. Where everything you need is in the palm of your hand
  • The facility even offers Torch which is a targeting system Pro-Bowlers use to be more accurate
  • Explore our wide variety of bowling balls in our ProShop.The ball review video on the right is one of many bowling balls we have in stock.
  • Relax in our lodge while enjoying beer, alcohol, and a Snack bar.
  • Come Join our Leagues that we offer every Monday-Saturday.
  • You can even enjoy our Cosmic Bowling during the weekend on Saturday from 5 PM to 9 PM.
